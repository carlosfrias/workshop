# Sample Prompts

Ready-to-use prompts for interacting with each domain. No command-line knowledge needed — just type the prompt naturally.

## Vault (Study & Knowledge)

- "Start a study session for Week {N}" — Sets up a study session with the right material
- "Summarize what I've learned about {topic}" — Pulls together insights from previous sessions
- "What's next in the curriculum?" — Checks where you are and what to study next
- "Connect ICM Layer {N} to {concept}" — Makes connections between layers and other ideas
- "Create study notes for {paper section}" — Captures understanding of specific paper content

## Workshop (Code & Implementation)

- "Build an ICM workspace for {use case}" — Creates a new workspace with proper structure
- "Create a stage contract for {stage description}" — Writes a CONTEXT.md with Inputs/Process/Outputs
- "Test the pipeline from stage {N}" — Runs a stage and checks output
- "Add a review gate after stage {N}" — Inserts human checkpoint documentation
- "Convert this monolithic prompt into ICM stages" — Decomposes a prompt into stages

## General

- "What is ICM?" — Explains Interpretable Context Methodology
- "Explain the 5-layer hierarchy" — Breaks down CLAUDE.md → CONTEXT.md → Stage → Reference → Working
- "How do I study effectively here?" — Suggests session format and study approach
- "Switch to vault" / "Switch to workshop" — Routes to the appropriate domain
