## Lessons

1. **The regression was a config change, not a logic bug.** The classifier logic was never broken — it was the default route that changed. This dramatically reduced the fix from 6–8 hours to 30 minutes once identified.
2. **Bisection parallelism is highly efficient.** 7 nodes tested 7 commits simultaneously, reducing wall-clock time from hours to minutes.
3. **Prompt capture is critical.** Without capturing the exact prompts that drove decisions, future refinement is impossible.
4. **doc-standards needed update.** The old type-based model scattered context. Issue-centric homes solve this.
5. **PI-intercom is preferred over SSH.** All lab nodes had pi-intercom running; dispatch was seamless.
